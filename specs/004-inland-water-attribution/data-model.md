# Data Model: Inland Water Attribution

**Phase 1 Output** | **Date**: 2026-03-02  
**Purpose**: Define entities, relationships, validation rules, and transitions for inland-water region ownership.

---

## 1) OwnershipCell

**Purpose**: Canonical per-zone ownership state used by region consumers.

**Fields**:
- `zoneX: int`
- `zoneY: int`
- `depthClass: enum { Land, Shallow, Deep }`
- `regionId: int` (`-1` when unassigned)
- `waterConnectivity: enum { NotWater, InlandWater, OceanConnectedWater }`

**Validation Rules**:
- Land cells preserve existing pre-attribution ownership.
- `waterConnectivity = NotWater` when `depthClass = Land`.

---

## 2) WaterBody

**Purpose**: Connected component of water zones analyzed as one attribution unit.

**Fields**:
- `waterBodyId: int`
- `zoneCount: int`
- `waterConnectivity: enum { InlandWater, OceanConnectedWater }`
- `adjacentRegionIds: Set<int>`

**Validation Rules**:
- `waterConnectivity` is uniform across zones in the component.
- Ocean-connected bodies are not attributed by this feature.

---

## 3) AttributionVote

**Purpose**: Deterministic scoring of candidate region owners for one inland water body.

**Fields**:
- `waterBodyId: int`
- `candidateRegionId: int`
- `sharedBorderEdges: int`

**Validation Rules**:
- `sharedBorderEdges > 0` for valid candidate.
- Winning candidate selected by max `sharedBorderEdges`, tie-break lowest `candidateRegionId`.

---

## 4) InlandAttributionResult

**Purpose**: Output summary of the attribution pass.

**Fields**:
- `attributedWaterZoneCount: int`
- `unassignedInlandWaterZoneCount: int`
- `attributedWaterBodyCount: int`
- `unassignedWaterBodyCount: int`
- `changedRegionIds: Set<int>`

**Validation Rules**:
- All counts non-negative.
- Attribution deterministic for same inputs.

---

## 5) RegionSummary

**Purpose**: Region-level aggregate metrics after attribution.

**Fields**:
- `regionId: int`
- `landAreaZones: int`
- `inlandWaterAreaZones: int`
- `totalAreaZones: int`

**Validation Rules**:
- `totalAreaZones = landAreaZones + inlandWaterAreaZones`.
- Land area remains unchanged by this feature.

---

## State Transitions

1. **Baseline**: land ownership generated by existing proto-region logic.
2. **Connectivity Categorization**: all water zones tagged inland or ocean-connected.
3. **Body Grouping**: water connectivity components built.
4. **Attribution**: inland bodies attributed to a winning adjacent region when available.
5. **Summary Update**: region metrics include inland water totals.

---

## Relationships

- `OwnershipCell` records are grouped into `WaterBody` components.
- Each inland `WaterBody` produces one or more `AttributionVote` records.
- Winning vote updates `OwnershipCell.regionId` for that water body.
- `InlandAttributionResult` and `RegionSummary` are derived from final ownership grid.
